Custom Jewelers of Richardson is a family owned & operated shop serving the Richardson community since 1976.

Our specialty is "recycling" your jewelry into a new custom design. Let us help you create a new piece of jewelry using your old gold and stones. We have a full inventory of designs in Sterling Silver, 14k Gold, 18k Gold, and Platinum. All jewelry repair is performed on state of the art equipment, by our in-house metal smiths.
